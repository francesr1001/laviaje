MANIWAYA ISLAND








AN AFFORDABLE TRAVEL DESTINATION

Maniwaya Island is located at Sta. Cruz Marinduque, the island is not that known that's why it is one of the hidden islands slash paradise of our country. To get to the island, if without a car you may commute and ride a bus to a certain bus station. After, it is required to ride a pump boat to get to the Island which adds a new experience for you. When you went to the Island, there are multiple resorts that accommodate travelers where you can pick your choice in. There is some travel agency that I find before that offers to let joiners travel that will pick you up in manila back and forth with a low budget you can afford!







#MANIWAYA'S_REGGAE_NIGHT

THE EXTRA FUN IN MANIWAYA!




In the nighttime, the Wawie's Beach Resort which I want to visit in Maniwaya is presenting a Reggae performance and fire performance as well. I find out that going to Maniwaya Island will not just make you see the great scenery such as white sand, blue sea, blue sky, and more but the place is also open and friendly for travelers who visit the Island.
