THE TOWN OF BLOSSOM
AKOT, BENGUET






In Atok, Benguet the climate was cold since it is also at a high point. Benguet is known for beautiful mountains and rice terraces, but Benguet is also popular in the field of botany. I always love flowers and I as well take a picture of them whenever I see flowers that it makes me love Benguet more. The scenery of the places as well is splendid that will yearn you to visit the said place.








BLOOM WITH BENGUET!

Northern Blossom and Sakura Park are on of the best attraction in Benguet that showcase different types of flowers and plants. The flowers are not the only travelers going to but also the scenery of the mountain and the relaxing climate within the area.









#STARS_AND_FLOWERS

In some certain months, the Milky Way's Galaxy is visible and illuminated the sky of Benguet in which people who fancy Astronomy and beyond bodies visited the Benguet. Since the place is in provincial place and the in the highest point, it is easy to see the stars and take a photo using quality-cam. It is indeed not only nature that Benguet is proud of, but also the mesmerizing phenomenon that can be seen in Benguet.
