THE VIGAN CITY






THE HISTORIC CITY

Why Calle Crisologo, Vigan?

I have always wanted to visit Vigan City since then, whenever I see the places in the picture, I always wonder what it looks like when I am there. Calle Crisologo is the one I want to visit in Vigan because going there seems like going back in time as well. It is said that Vigan came from the word Bee Gan which means a beautiful shore, but I am sure that it deserves to be called "Vigan" even I haven't been there yet.




Cafe Uno Heritage

As a person who loves coffee a lot. Just like the place, I also want to try Vigan's coffee shop called Cafe Uno Heritage which offers coffee and other appetizers. While drinking my coffee, it will feel good to look around the scenery of Calle Crisologo.
